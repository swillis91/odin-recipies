Lasagna

Description"

Lasagna is an Italian food that is a layered pasta dish. It is one of my favorite Italian dishes.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 jar pasta sauce
  • 400 g ricotta cheese
  • 200g low fat mozzerella cheese
  • 1lb ground beef

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Brown ground beef in a skillet; drain.
  3. While browning beef, add lasagna noodles to boiling water; cook until al dente.
  4. Coat bottom of pan with 1/4C pasta sauce, then assemble lasagna: pasta, ricotta, beef, sauce)
